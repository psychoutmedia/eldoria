# The Shattered Realms MUD - Bestiary & Monster Guide

This document provides full details on the wandering monsters and legendary bosses that inhabit the Shattered Realms. From the ethereal wisps of the starting chambers to the supreme guardians of the Archmage Sanctum, every creature is detailed here.

> **For Server 2 (Neo Kyoto, rooms 201-300):** see the companion file **`Beasts of Neo Kyoto.md`**, which catalogs the 26 Neo Kyoto monsters and 5 Tier 3.1 bosses with their realm-specific resist profiles, damage types, and signature mechanics.

## 🧠 Monster AI & Wandering Behavior

Monsters in the Shattered Realms are not static. They possess various AI behaviors:

- **Passive**: These creatures will never attack players first. They often carry useful consumables or lore items.
- **Neutral**: These creatures only attack if provoked. They are often sturdy and provide good experience.
- **Aggressive**: These predators will attack any player who enters their room. Travelers should be wary!
- **Boss**: Unique, powerful entities that guard specific locations. They have massive health pools and special attacks.

**Wandering**: Most non-boss monsters will periodically move between adjacent rooms within their zone, creating a dynamic and unpredictable environment.
